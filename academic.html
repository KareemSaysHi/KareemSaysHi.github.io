Academic Work

Expository Papers

  • On Dimension-Free \(L^p\) bounds for Maximal Functions
    • My junior independent work at Princeton, advised by Assaf Naor. I wrote an exposition of work of Stein, Bourgain, Carbery, and Muller, on dimension independent estimates for the Hardy-Littlewood maximal function associated to a convex body.
  • A Proof of The Gauss-Bonnet Theorem
    • My final paper for my Junior Seminar (MAT 981: Geometric Analysis) at Princeton.

Research Papers

  • Congruence Classes of Simplex Structures in Finite Field Vector Spaces (SMALL REU 2024)
    • My primary focus at SMALL 2024! With Tim Cheek, Joe Cooper, Pico Gilman, Vismay Sharan, Jenna Shuffelton, and Marie-Hélène Tomé, advised by Eyvi Palsson and Alex Iosevich.
    • I presented this work with Vismay Sharan at JMM 2025 and at the Young Mathematicians Conference 2024.
  • On the Density of Low Lying Zeros of a Large Family of Automorphic \(L\)-functions (SMALL REU 2024)
    • With Tim Cheek, Pico Gilman, and Marie-Hélène Tomé, advised by Steven J. Miller
    • I presented this work with my collaborators at JMM 2025.
  • Colorings of Symmetric Unions and Partial Knots (Polymath Jr REU 2022)
    • A online REU project I worked on the summer before I started at Princeton. With Ben Clingenpeel, Zongzheng Dai, Gabriel Diraviam, Krishnendu Kar, Ziyun Liu, Teo Miklethun, Haritha Nagampoozhy, Michael Perry, Moses Samuelson-Lynn, Eli Seamans, Ana Wright, Nicole Xie, and Ruiqi Zou, advised by Alexander Zupan
