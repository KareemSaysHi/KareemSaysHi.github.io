Fun Stuff

4Desmos - a 4D VR graphing calculator

A fun project me and Colby Riley are working on! We started this at HackPrinceton 2025 and won the education category. Check out the devpost and github! More to come this summer :D

Card Memorization

I started learning how to speed-memorize a deck of cards in March of 2025! My current record is 4:53, using the Person Action Object system. I also made a little Windows application to time and check an attempt.

Animation: The Duck Potluck

As my final project for Princeton's VIS220: Digital Animation, I animated a short film about a couple ducks at a potluck. This probably is 150 hours of work for a 3 minute video :) Watch it here!

Penguins Online

The Fine Hall common room has this Don't Break the Ice knockoff lovingly known as Penguins. I made a online version of it that you can play here!

Picnic Basket Puzzles

I'm big into 3D printed puzzles, and I've designed a couple picnic basket packing puzzles heavily inspired by the Akaki's Picnic puzzles. I also presented a RadLab on puzzle making at Princeton's StudioLab in 2024. When I get around to it, I'll put up the files here.
